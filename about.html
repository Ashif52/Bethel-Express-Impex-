Our History

A Decade of Logistics Excellence.

Founded in 2014, Bethel Express & Impex was born from a vision to simplify the complexities of international trade and pharmaceutical distribution.

We recognized that while the world was becoming more connected, the barriers to reliable, cost-effective, and compliant shipping remained high—especially for specialized sectors like healthcare. We set out to bridge this gap by combining global shipping infrastructure with boutique-level attention to detail.
