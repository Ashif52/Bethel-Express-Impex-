How Pricing Works

Slab-Based Rates: Per-KG rates are tiered by weight range, ensuring fair pricing for all shipment sizes.
Fuel Surcharge: Applied as a percentage on the freight cost to account for fluctuating fuel prices.
GST: Goods and Services Tax applied as per government regulations on the total service value.
Documents: Flat-rate pricing for document shipments regardless of weight.

Important Note

Rates shown are indicative estimates based on standard shipment parameters. Final charges may vary after pickup and physical inspection. For exact quotes, please contact our team.
