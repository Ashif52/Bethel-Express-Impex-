How can I track my international shipment?

You can track any shipment using the tracking ID provided at the time of booking. Simply visit our Tracking page, enter your unique Bethel Tracking ID, and get real-time updates on your parcel's location and status.

What are your delivery timeframes for the USA?

Delivery to the USA typically takes 3-5 business days for Express shipments and 7-10 business days for Economy shipments. Actual times may vary based on customs clearance and destination city.

Do you handle pharmaceutical shipments?

Yes! Bethel Express & Impex is a TN licensed agency specializing in pharmaceutical wholesale distribution. We maintain cold chain integrity and comply with all regulatory requirements for medicine transport.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and corporate accounts for regular customers. Custom billing arrangements can be made for enterprise clients.
